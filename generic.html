More About Me

My name is Giulia Martins and I am a third-year undergrad student from University of South Florida. I am obtaining a degree in Bachelors of Science with a major in Computer Science and pursuing a career in Front-end Development. I have a strong passion for technology and designing websites. I learned how to use HTML and CSS during high school and I became fascinated with the languages. During my last year, I was introduced to Bootstrap and how to make a website more responsive. This type of CSS became my favorite to use when designing each of projects. Even though I have 6 years of experience with HTML and CSS (3 with Bootstrap), I have yet to expand my knowledge and skill when it comes to Front-end Development. I hope through my last few semesters at USF and obtaining an internship, I can become fully knowledgable in the career that I soon want to obtain.

My passion for technology came from my father who has a certificate in IT. He is knowledgable in PHP, HTML, CSS, C, and Python. He taught me how to build my first website and understand the hardware of a personal computer. I would not be as knowledgable as I am today without my father, especially my Web Design teacher. I am grateful for those you have supported me in this journey. I can wait what the future has in store for me.
